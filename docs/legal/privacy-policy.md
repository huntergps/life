# Privacy Policy -- Galapagos Wildlife

Last updated: April 1, 2026

## What data we collect
- **Account data**: Email address (when you create an account)
- **Sighting data**: Photos, GPS coordinates, timestamps, notes (when you record sightings)
- **Profile data**: Display name, country, bio, avatar (optional)
- **Usage data**: App preferences (language, theme)

## How we use your data
- To provide the app's core functionality (species catalog, sighting recording, favorites)
- To sync your data across devices
- To improve species identification accuracy (with your consent)

## Data storage
- Data is stored in Supabase (cloud database hosted on AWS)
- Local data is stored on your device via SQLite
- Images are stored in Supabase Storage

## Data sharing
- We do NOT sell your personal data
- We do NOT share your data with third parties for advertising
- Sighting data may be used anonymously for conservation research (with your consent)

## Your rights
- **Access**: View all your data in the app
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings
- **Export**: Export your sightings as CSV

## Data retention
- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- After account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Children's privacy
- The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13

## Contact
- Email: admin@galapagos.tech

## Changes
- We may update this policy. Changes will be noted with a new "Last updated" date.
