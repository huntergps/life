Privacy Policy

Galapagos Wildlife — Last updated: April 1, 2026

What data we collect

  • Account data: Email address (when you create an account)
  • Sighting data: Photos, GPS coordinates, timestamps, notes (when you record wildlife sightings)
  • Profile data: Display name, country, bio, avatar (optional)
  • Usage data: App preferences (language, theme)
  • Purchase data: Purchase status is managed by Apple/Google; we only store whether premium features are unlocked

How we use your data

  • To provide the app's core functionality (species catalog, sighting recording, favorites)
  • To sync your data across devices when you have an account
  • To improve species identification accuracy (with your consent)

Data storage

  • Cloud data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure)
  • Local data is stored on your device via SQLite for offline access
  • Photos are stored in Supabase Storage (encrypted at rest)
  • We do not store payment information — all purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play

Data sharing

  • We do NOT sell your personal data
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties for advertising
  • We do NOT use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • Sighting data may be used anonymously for conservation research (with your consent)

Your rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Delete Account
  • Export: Export your sightings as CSV from the sightings screen
  • Portability: Your sighting data can be exported at any time

Data retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • After account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Anonymous, aggregated data (e.g., species sighting counts) may be retained for conservation purposes

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

Third-party services

  • Supabase: Authentication and data storage (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: In-app purchases and app distribution
  • iNaturalist: Some species images sourced from iNaturalist (Creative Commons)

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be noted with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
