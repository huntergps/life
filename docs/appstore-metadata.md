## Version 1.0.0

### Description (English)

Galapagos Wildlife is the ultimate offline guide to the fauna of the Galapagos Islands. Built for tourists, naturalists, and nature lovers.

SPECIES CHECKLIST
Start with the Big 15 — the most iconic species of Galapagos. Mark species as you spot them, track your progress, and earn the Galapagos Master certificate when you complete them all! Customize your list by adding or removing species.

COMPLETE SPECIES CATALOG
Browse 131 species across 6 categories: birds, reptiles, mammals, marine life, invertebrates, and spiders. Each species includes photos, scientific classification, habitat, conservation status, and more.

WORKS OFFLINE
Download once, use everywhere. The app works fully without internet — perfect for the Galapagos where connectivity is limited.

RECORD SIGHTINGS
Document your wildlife encounters with photos, GPS location, date, and notes. Your sightings sync across devices when you're back online.

AI SPECIES IDENTIFICATION
Take a photo and let on-device AI help identify the species. No internet required — the neural network runs entirely on your device.

INTERACTIVE MAP (Premium)
Explore 891 official visitor sites and 43 trails from the Galapagos National Park. See which species you can find at each location.

GAMIFICATION
Earn achievement badges, climb the leaderboard, and celebrate with confetti when you complete your checklist!

BILINGUAL
Full English and Spanish support throughout the app.
